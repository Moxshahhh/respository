Dal Batti

Dal Batti is a traditional Rajasthani dish that consists of two main components: Dal and Batti. Dal is a lentil-based curry made with various spices, while Batti is a type of round bread that is baked until it becomes crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. The dish is often served with ghee (clarified butter) and is a popular choice for festive occasions and special meals in Rajasthan.
